About Our Company

We Build Spaces That Inspire & Elevate Living

Ana Junubi General Construction Company is a trusted construction and interior design company based in Juba, South Sudan, specializing in high-quality building solutions, luxury interior designs, and professional painting services.

We are committed to delivering elegant, modern, and functional spaces that reflect our clients' vision and lifestyle. From residential homes to commercial properties, we provide construction and finishing services that combine beauty, durability, and excellence.
